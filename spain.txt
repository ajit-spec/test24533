Spain is a country located on the Iberian Peninsula in southwestern Europe.
Spain is known for its rich history, culture, and diverse landscapes, including the Pyrenees Mountains, the Mediterranean coastline, and the cities of Barcelona, Madrid, and Seville.
The official language of Spain is Spanish, also known as Castilian.
The country is famous for its delicious cuisine, including paella, tapas, and sangria.
Spain has a population of approximately 47 million people as of 2021.
